Montauk, New York

Montauk is one of the best vacation destinations in the country. It is located on the eastern end of the South Shore of Long Island. It is a large tourist destination making your experiene the best East Coast summer you've ever had. It has amazing beaches, resorts, restaurants, and more. Montauk is also known for the lighthouse located at the end of the point. The famous slogan Montauk embodies is "The End of the World.""
