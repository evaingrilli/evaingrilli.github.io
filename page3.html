Montauk, New York

Montauk is one of the best vacation destinations in the country. It is located on the eastern end of the South Shore of Long Island. It is a large tourist destination making your experiene the best East Coast summer you've ever had. It has amazing beaches, resorts, restaurants, and more. Montauk is also known for the lighthouse located at the end of the point. The famous slogan Montauk embodies is "The End of the World." Some of the best beaches are Ditch Plains, Sunset Beach, Hither Hills State Park, and South Lake Beach. In town you can grab food at some of the best restaurants, for example, Harvest on Fort Pond, Sammy's, The Point, and Surf Lodge. Surfing is also very common in Montauk, so there are many surf shops where you can purchase surfboards and other gear. Air and Speed, Montauk Surf & Sports, and Sunset Surf Shack are a few of the most common ones.
